We need identify which option equals the Lebesgue integral of the derivative of a monotone increasing function.

For a monotone increasing function, \(f'\) exists almost everywhere. A standard result says that if

\[
E=\{x\in [a,b]: f'(x)\text{ exists}\},
\]

then

\[
\int_a^b f'(x)\,dx = m(f(E)).
\]

Thus the correct option is E.
