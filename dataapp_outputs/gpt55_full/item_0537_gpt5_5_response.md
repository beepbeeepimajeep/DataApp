We need the amount of vinegar for a larger batch, keeping the same ketchup-to-vinegar ratio.

Each serving uses 7.5 tablespoons of ketchup. With 52.5 tablespoons of ketchup:

\[
52.5 \div 7.5 = 7
\]

So the batch is 7 servings. Each serving uses 6 tablespoons of vinegar:

\[
7 \times 6 = 42
\]
