Let the first two integers be \(a\) and \(b\). We need the third integer.

The third integer is

\[
a+b
\]

Let \(s=a+b\). Then the five integers are:

\[
a,\ b,\ s,\ 2s,\ 4s
\]

Their sum is:

\[
s+s+2s+4s=8s
\]

Given the sum is \(176\),

\[
8s=176
\]

\[
s=22
\]

So the third integer is \(22\).

\[
\boxed{22}
\]
