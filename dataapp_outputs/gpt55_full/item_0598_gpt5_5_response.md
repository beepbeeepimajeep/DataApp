We need diagonalize the quadratic form and match it to an option.

Take the non-degenerate substitution
\[
x_1=y_1+\frac12y_2-y_3,\qquad x_2=y_3,\qquad x_3=y_1-\frac12y_2+2y_3.
\]
Its substitution matrix has determinant \(1\), so it is non-degenerate.

Substituting into
\[
-4x_1x_2+2x_1x_3+2x_2x_3
\]
gives
\[
2y_1^2-\frac12y_2^2+4y_3^2.
\]
This matches option G.
