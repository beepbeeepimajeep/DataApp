We need the constant of proportionality and then the room area for a given BTU capacity.

Let cooling capacity \(C\) be directly proportional to area \(A\):

\[
C=kA
\]

Using \(C=6440\) BTUs and \(A=280\) square feet:

\[
k=\frac{6440}{280}=23
\]

So the constant is \(23\) BTUs per square foot.

For \(C=9430\) BTUs:

\[
9430=23A
\]

\[
A=\frac{9430}{23}=410
\]

So the room area is \(410\) square feet.

Verification: There are exactly 4 answers, in order: constant, units, room size, units.

\[
\boxed{23,\ \text{BTUs per square foot},\ 410,\ \text{square feet}}
\]
