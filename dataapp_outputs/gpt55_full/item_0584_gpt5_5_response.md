We need the amplitude and the frequency of the oscillation.

Given  
\(y = 7z\cos(12\pi wt)\)

(a) The furthest distance from equilibrium is the amplitude:  
\(7z\) cm.

(b) Compare \(12\pi wt\) with \(2\pi ft\):  
\[
2\pi f = 12\pi w
\]
\[
f = 6w
\]
So the mass completes \(6w\) oscillations in 1 second.

Verification: There are exactly 2 answers, in the order requested: distance, number of oscillations.

\boxed{7z,\ 6w}
