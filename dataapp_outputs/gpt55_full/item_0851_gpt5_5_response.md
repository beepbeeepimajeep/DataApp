We need to check which ordered pairs satisfy \(y=-\frac{5}{8}x\).

- A. \((-32,22)\): \(-\frac{5}{8}(-32)=20\), not \(22\). Not a solution.
- B. \((0,0)\): \(-\frac{5}{8}(0)=0\). Solution.
- C. \((-24,15)\): \(-\frac{5}{8}(-24)=15\). Solution.
- D. \((32,-16)\): \(-\frac{5}{8}(32)=-20\), not \(-16\). Not a solution.

\(\boxed{\text{B and C}}\)
