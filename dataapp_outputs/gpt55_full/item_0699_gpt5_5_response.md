We need the coefficient \(c\) and exponent \(e\) in the form \(c/x^e\).

\[
x^3\left(\frac{1}{7}x^4\right)(42x^{-10})
= \frac{42}{7}x^{3+4-10}
= 6x^{-3}
= \frac{6}{x^3}
\]

Thus \(c=6\) and \(e=3\).

Verification: exactly 2 answers are needed, in the order coefficient \(c\), exponent \(e\).
